Our ISO 27001 Services

At NSU Secure Solutions, we provide an end-to-end ISO 27001 implementation experience, making your path to certification simple, smooth, and tailored to your business needs. Each step is designed to ensure zero disruption and maximum security posture improvement.

  1. Initial Consultation:
    We begin by understanding your business, data risks, current practices, and vision. We help you define a clear scope for your ISMS.
  2. Risk Assessment & Gap Analysis:
    Our experts identify vulnerabilities and compare your current controls against ISO 27001 requirements.
  3. Policy Design & Documentation:
    We help draft ISMS policies, controls, and operating procedures that align with your goals and ISO 27001 standards.
  4. Implementation Support:
    We guide your team to adopt, apply, and integrate new security controls into everyday operations.
  5. Internal Audit & Corrective Actions:
    A full mock audit to uncover compliance gaps and support in resolving non-conformities.
  6. Training & Awareness:
    We conduct employee training sessions to build awareness about information security practices.
  7. Certification Assistance:
    We assist in choosing the right certifying body and ensure you're fully audit-ready.
  8. Post-Certification Support:
    Continuous improvement, yearly reviews, and support to maintain your ISO 27001 status.
