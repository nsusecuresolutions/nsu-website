FAQ PCI DSS CERTIFICATION

PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) is a global standard that ensures organizations securely handle cardholder data. It helps prevent data breaches, ensures customer trust, and is mandatory for businesses that process credit/debit cards.

Any business that stores, processes, or transmits credit or debit card information must comply. This includes e-commerce sites, payment gateway providers, retail shops using PoS systems, SaaS platforms handling card data, and financial institutions like banks and NBFCs.
